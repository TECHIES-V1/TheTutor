## Production Notes

### Color Reference (for AI image generation prompts)
- Background: #0a0a0a (near black)
- Primary gold: #d4af37 (classic gold) / #e8b930 (bright gold)
- Text: #f5f0e3 (warm cream)
- Muted text: rgba(245,240,227,0.5)
- Card surfaces: semi-transparent with subtle white/gold borders, inner glow, backdrop blur
- Shadows: neumorphic — a light source from top-left creates both highlight and shadow edges

### Styling Keywords (for AI prompts)
- "neo-surface" = glassmorphic card with raised look, subtle border, backdrop blur
- "card-leather" = slightly textured card with dual shadow (raised + inset)
- "skeuo-gold" = 3D gold gradient button with a diagonal shine overlay strip, looks physically raised
- "neo-inset" = sunken input/container, like pressed into the surface

### Music & Sound
- Lo-fi or cinematic ambient beat throughout

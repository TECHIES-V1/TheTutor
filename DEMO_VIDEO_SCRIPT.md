## Production Notes

- **Music:** Lo-fi or cinematic ambient beat, building in intensity during the generation scene (0:18–0:28), resolving at CTA
- **Pacing:** Fast but not frantic. Let the generation scene breathe — it's the hero moment
- **Transitions:** Smooth crossfades between sections, quick cuts only during the problem montage
- **Text overlays:** Minimal, gold on black, only to reinforce key phrases
- **Screen recordings:** Use the actual app at 1.5x speed where needed, with cursor highlights
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum, 4K preferred for quality perception
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 for YouTube/website, also export 9:16 vertical cut for social

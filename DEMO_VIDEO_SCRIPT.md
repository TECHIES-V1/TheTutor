## Production Notes

- **Music:** Lo-fi or cinematic ambient beat, building in intensity during generation (0:16–0:25), softening during podcast mode (0:25–0:33) to let the read-aloud concept land, resolving at CTA
- **Podcast mode scene is the differentiator** — let the gold highlight animation breathe. Show 3-4 lines being highlighted in sequence. This is what makes TheTutor different from every other platform
- **AI voice scene:** Capture actual mic activation → voice input → streaming response → TTS playback. The voice loop is the wow moment
- **Pacing:** Fast but not frantic. The two voice-powered scenes (podcast + AI assistant) should feel calm and intentional
- **Transitions:** Smooth crossfades between sections, quick cuts only during the problem montage
- **Text overlays:** Minimal, gold on black, only to reinforce key phrases
- **Screen recordings:** Use the actual app at 1.5x speed for chat/generation, real-time for podcast mode
- **Sound design:** Consider letting actual TTS audio peek through briefly during the podcast mode scene (lower music, let the voice come in for 1-2 seconds)
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum, 4K preferred for quality perception
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 for YouTube/website, also export 9:16 vertical cut for social
